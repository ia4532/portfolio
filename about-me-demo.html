About Me

My name is Ida Quan and I am currently attending at California State University East Bay. I am a transfer student so I can't really say if I am a junior or a senior so lets just say I am a super senior. Before I transfered, I previously studied at Laney, Alameda, and Merritt (Peralta Community Colleges). My major is Multimedia and I am currently working towards on getting a Bachelor of Arts degree. I had changed majors many times because of my parents' disapprovals. I had changed from Animation to Graphic Design, Spacial Art (Ceramics), Fashion Designer, and Cosmetology.

Although, I do admit that I'm glad I settled with Multimedia. Multimedia lets me work from traditional media (papers) to digital (Photoshop, Illustrator, Painter, Atom, etc.). I didn't have to dedicate myself to a piece of paper every day if I were to draw. Besides drawing, I can even make a photo look like I actually painted it in Painter without having to actually paint it. After experiencing many softwares, I do not regret choosing Multimedia as my major.
