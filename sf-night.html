This project really got me thinking for the longest. I was supposed to find an event that is happening at a specific time (evening or night) and day in San Francisco. Of course, it was during November and not much is happening. Then, I decided to create something based on the event I went to (Kimono Day) during the past year. I created this design with Illustrator and it was pretty fun to do. All the images (people, tower, stairs, etd) were created with only four shapes: triangles, squares, rectangles, and circles. Since this event didn't exactly happen during the evening, I decided to make it seem like it did by having to ladies bowing to each other saying "Thank you for your hard work". That phrase is used by the Japanese to acknowledge others for their hard work.
