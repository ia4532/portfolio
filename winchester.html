For this assignment, it needed to be in black and white. Not only does it have to be in black and white, it also need to have a creepy, mysterious, or haunted etc. feeling. What I did with this image of the Winchester Mystery House, I decided to make it look haunted yet antique. I increased the brightness and contrast. After fiddling with the brightness and contrast, I noticed that the white were too bright. What I did to fix the issue was to add a layer of Hue/Saturation over the whole image. With the Hue/Saturation, I managed to create a blue tint that porrays both haunted and antique feeling.
